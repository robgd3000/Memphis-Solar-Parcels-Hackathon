Purpose

This is a map that shows available parcels of land in the city of Memphis. The purpose of this map is to show the feasibility and possible benefits of putting solar panels in the unused parcels.

The average Tennessee household uses about 41.5 kilowatt hours a day. The following equation shows that two tenths of an acre of solar panels can offset the energy usage of 10 houses by 20%.

41.5 x 100 x 10 x .2 = 8300 Sq. Ft or .2 Acres
